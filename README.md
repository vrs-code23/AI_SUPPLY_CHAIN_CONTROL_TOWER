# 🚚 AI Supply Chain Control Tower

An end-to-end **AI-Powered Supply Chain Intelligence Platform** built using **Python, MySQL, Machine Learning, Streamlit, and Gemini AI**. The platform transforms operational supply chain data into actionable business insights through predictive analytics, risk intelligence, optimization engines, and a GenAI-powered decision support system.

## 🔥 Key Capabilities

* 📈 **Demand Forecasting Engine** using Machine Learning to predict future demand patterns and support inventory planning.
* 📦 **Inventory Optimization Module** for intelligent reorder recommendations and stock-level monitoring.
* 🏭 **Supplier Risk Analytics Engine** that evaluates vendors based on lead time, quality performance, on-time delivery rate, and cost efficiency.
* 🚚 **Logistics Delay Prediction Model** to proactively identify potential delivery disruptions and operational bottlenecks.
* 🤖 **AI Supply Chain Copilot** powered by Gemini AI for natural language querying, operational insights, and decision support.
* 📊 **Interactive Streamlit Dashboard** providing centralized visibility into critical supply chain KPIs.
